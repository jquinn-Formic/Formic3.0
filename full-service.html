How It Works

From inquiry
to running

Week 1

Application Scoping

Our engineers assess your production line, throughput requirements, product specs, and floor constraints. FormicFAST matches your application to a pre-engineered solution within days — not weeks of custom engineering.

Weeks 2–4

System Configuration & Testing

Your system is built, programmed, and fully tested off-site before it ships. Pre-configured for your specific products, packaging formats, and throughput targets. What arrives on your floor is ready to run.

Weeks 4–6

Installation & Commissioning

Field Service Engineers handle the full install. Operator training — bilingual — is included. We don't leave until your team is confident and your system is hitting contracted performance rates.

Ongoing

24/7 Support & Optimization

Remote monitoring, proactive diagnostics, preventive maintenance visits, immediate corrective response — all included. Your system improves through continuous software updates. Performance SLAs are in your contract.
